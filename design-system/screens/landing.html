What it does

Everything your business runs on,
handled automatically

Agents handle the work. You set the rules, review the results, and stay in control.

Customer Engagement
Monitor your inbox, classify inbound messages, draft personalised replies, score leads, and route hot prospects to your CRM — automatically, 24/7.
Administration
Process invoices, extract data from documents, update spreadsheets, schedule meetings, and file everything in the right place without lifting a finger.
Research
Track competitors, monitor news, compile market summaries, and deliver weekly briefings — sourced, structured, and sent to you on a schedule.
How it works

From setup to running in minutes

No engineering team required. Build workflows visually, deploy agents, and let them work.

1
Define your workflow
Connect nodes in the visual builder — triggers, agents, logic, and outputs.
2
Configure each agent
Set the goal, inputs, and model. Add approval gates where human oversight matters.
3
Deploy and run
Agents run on schedule or on demand. Every action is logged and explained.
4
Review and refine
See what each agent did, why, and what it produced. Adjust and improve over time.
